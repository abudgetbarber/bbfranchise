Franchise Fee
One-time fee of ₹2 Lakhs to join the Budget Barber family. This grants you the rights to use our brand, systems, and support for the contract period.
🪑
Setup & Interior Cost
₹4–6 Lakhs for interior, furniture, equipment, branding, signage, and initial stock. Varies based on city and salon size (200–400 sq ft typical).
🔄
Working Capital
₹1.5–2.5 Lakhs for 3 months of running expenses — salaries, rent, utilities, and consumables — while you build your customer base.
📋
Monthly Royalty
8% of gross monthly revenue. This covers ongoing access to our brand, technology platform, marketing support, and training programs.
✅
What's Included in the Package
  • Complete interior design plan + execution support
  • All branded equipment and furniture at negotiated rates
  • 10-day staff training at our training center
  • App & digital tools setup (booking, billing, CRM)
  • Launch marketing — social media, local promotions
  • Dedicated relationship manager for first 90 days

Days 1–7: Documentation
Franchise agreement signing, payment of franchise fee, location agreement, GST registration support, and license checklist provided.
🔨
Days 8–35: Interior & Setup
Our empaneled interior team executes the design. Branded equipment delivered. Signage installed. Tech setup: POS, app, billing, CRM — all configured.
🎓
Days 20–30: Training
10-day training for barbers at our center — haircuts, styling, hygiene, customer service. Owner gets business ops training separately. All in Telugu/Hindi/local language.
🎉
Days 45–60: Launch!
Pre-launch WhatsApp campaign, social media blitz, in-store offers. Our team is present on Day 1 to ensure smooth operations. You're live!
